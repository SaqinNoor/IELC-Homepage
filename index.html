Legacy of Excellence

Since its inception on February 29, 2016, Ideal English Language Club has consistently stood out as one of the most prominent organizations, not only within Ideal School and College but throughout the entire country.

Founded under the guidance of Abul Kalam Azad, the club's first Moderator, this club has seen seven dedicated Executive Committees diligently serve during their respective tenures.
